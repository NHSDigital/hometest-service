{% call details({ summaryText: "What's in the kit" }) %}

The test kit normally includes:

  • Instructions
  • Blood sample tube
  • Alcohol wipe
  • Lancets
  • Plaster
  • Transport bag
  • Security seal sticker
  • Box with pre-paid return postage
{{ image({ src: "/images/self-sample-kit-contents.jpg", sizes: "(max-width: 768px) 100vw, 66vw", srcset: "/images/self-sample-kit-contents.jpg 600w, /images/self-sample-kit-contents.jpg 1000w", alt: "Image showing the contents of the kit", caption: "Kit contents." }) }} {% endcall %} {% call details({ summaryText: "Tips before you start" }) %}

These tips can help you collect your blood sample:

  • Drink a big glass of water at least 30 minutes before you do the test
  • Do the test after a shower or bath
  • Put your hand into a bowl of warm water a few minutes before doing the test
  • Swing your arms in a circular motion. Or clench your hands into a fist a few times
  • Read the instructions in full before starting

Step 3: Prick your finger

Step 5: Fill the tube

Step 6: Seal and mix the tube
